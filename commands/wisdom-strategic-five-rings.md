<process>
## Step 1: Understand the Challenge
What situation or endeavor is the user facing?
- What's the goal?
- Who/what are the obstacles?
- What's at stake?

## Step 2: Earth Ring Analysis - Foundation
Examine the fundamentals:
- What are the basic requirements for success here?
- Is your foundation solid?
- What fundamentals are you neglecting?
- Have you mastered the basics before attempting the advanced?

**Earth principle:** Build on solid ground. Master basics before complexity.

## Step 3: Water Ring Analysis - Adaptability
Examine flexibility:
- Are you rigid in your approach?
- Can you adapt to changing conditions?
- What would it look like to "be like water"â€”taking the shape of the container?
- Are you forcing or flowing?

**Water principle:** Adapt your shape to the situation. Be formless yet powerful.

## Step 4: Fire Ring Analysis - Decisive Action
Examine your engagement:
- When is aggressive, decisive action needed?
- Are you being too passive?
- What would bold action look like here?
- Is hesitation costing you?

**Fire principle:** When the moment comes, act with total commitment.

## Step 5: Wind Ring Analysis - External Awareness
Examine your knowledge of others:
- Do you understand your opponent/competition/obstacle?
- What are others' strategies in this domain?
- What do others know that you don't?
- Are you too inwardly focused?

**Wind principle:** Know others' ways to transcend them.

## Step 6: Void Ring Analysis - Transcendence
Examine the higher perspective:
- Can you let go of fixed strategies?
- What does your intuition say when you quiet the mind?
- Where is your ego getting in the way?
- What would acting from "no-mind" look like?

**Void principle:** True mastery transcends technique. Act from emptiness.
</process>
